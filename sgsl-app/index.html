SgSL manual alphabet

SgSL fingerspelling follows the ASL manual alphabet, except T, which is modified locally (the ASL T handshape is offensive in Singapore). Click any letter to see it animated.

SgSL Studio is an independent learning aid, not an official SADeaf resource. SgSL blends Shanghainese Sign Language, ASL, SEE and local signs — learn from the community: Singapore Association for the Deaf · NTU SgSL Sign Bank · deaf.sg
